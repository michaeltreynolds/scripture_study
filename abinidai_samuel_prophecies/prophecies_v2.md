# Prophecies of Abinadi and Samuel the Lamanite — V2

A **verse-by-verse** enumeration of every distinct prophecy or description of the future. V1 compressed multi-verse passages into single entries, which caused important elements (e.g., Helaman 13:37's demonic infestation) to be buried. V2 splits each distinct prophetic claim into its own row.

> **Scope note**: Only *predictive* or *warning* statements are cataloged — not doctrinal teachings about the nature of God, the atonement, or the law of Moses, unless they include a "shall come to pass" element.

## V1 → V2 Changelog

| V1 ID | V2 IDs | What changed |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| S7 | **S17–S23** | "Lamentation in the day of poverty" split into 7 distinct prophecies. **S23 (surrounded by demons)** was completely hidden in V1 and is now the primary match for Mormon 1:19's sorceries/witchcrafts. |
| S5 | **S8–S13** | "Curse upon the land and riches" split into 6 entries covering the curse, slippery treasures, and riches cursed separately. |
| S6 | **S7, S14–S15** | "Casting out prophets" split: S7 is the trigger condition, S14–S15 cover the false-prophet contrast. |
| A1–A3 | A1–A6 | Abinadi's first appearance split verse by verse (was 3 entries, now 6). |
| A16–A23 | A16–A24 | Added A17–A18 (devil's power) and A24 (vengeance) which were omitted in V1. |
