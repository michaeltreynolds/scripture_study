# Prophecies of Abinadi and Samuel the Lamanite

A detailed enumeration of every prophecy or description of future events made by Abinadi and Samuel the Lamanite. Doctrinal teachings (e.g., the nature of Christ, the atonement) are distinguished from *predictive prophecies* about what would happen to the people.

---

| S6 | Casting out prophets leads to ripeness for destruction | Helaman 13:14, 24–26 | "When ye shall cast out the righteous from among you, then shall ye be ripe for destruction...ye do cast out the prophets, and do mock them, and cast stones at them, and do slay them." |
| S7 | Lamentation in the day of poverty | Helaman 13:32–37 | "In the days of your poverty ye shall cry unto the Lord; and in vain shall ye cry...ye shall say: O that I had repented...our riches are gone from us...our swords are taken from us...all things are become slippery." |

| S9 | Houses left desolate; no place of refuge | Helaman 15:1–2 | "Except ye shall repent your houses shall be left unto you desolate...ye shall attempt to flee and there shall be no place for refuge." |
